Privacy Policy for ClearMyMail

Last Updated: 12/01/2024

Welcome to ClearMyMail, an application designed to enhance your email management experience by efficiently identifying and deleting unwanted or spam emails. This Privacy Policy explains how ClearMyMail, developed by Andi Ndoja, handles, uses, and protects any information that you provide while using the app.

1. Information Collection and Use

ClearMyMail accesses your Gmail account to perform the following functions:

  • Collecting email addresses from your emails.
  • Identifying and deleting emails based on your input from an uploaded Excel file.

The data collected by ClearMyMail includes:

  • Email addresses from your Gmail account.
  • Email headers and identifiers necessary for the functionality of the app.

The data collected is used exclusively for:

  • Compiling a list of email addresses and storing it in an Excel file for your review.
  • Deleting emails from your Gmail account based on your provided list of email addresses.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

ClearMyMail does not share any information with third parties. The data accessed by the application remains confidential and is only used for its intended functionality. Your data is not shared, sold, or distributed to any third-party entities.

3. Data Storage and Security

ClearMyMail operates locally on your device. No personal data is stored externally or transmitted to the developer or any third parties. All processing is conducted locally on your machine, ensuring maximum data security.
